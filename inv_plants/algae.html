Algae (filamentous algae)

Form:

Deciduous tree, 50+ feet tall, wide variety of moisture and light conditions.

Leaves:

Opposite, 4 to 6 inches across, 5-7 lobes, juice milky, few large teeth.

Fruits & Seeds:

Late spring, green to tan, double samara with 2 widely spread "wings".

Native Look alikes:

Sugar Maple (Acer saccharum)

Tier: 2

The size and extent of these species populations are currently being inventoried with limited geographic information available. This survey will assist with identifying future control targets as populations are defined.

Action: The inventory of these plants is ongoing. The approximate number of plants should be recorded keeping in mind population sizes may be highly variable.

Season Treated:

Any; Mature trees are cut down, while smaller trees are girdled or cut-stumped and treated with a highly concentrated herbicide solution.
